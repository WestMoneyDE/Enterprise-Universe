## 6. ANTI-DILUTION

### 6.1 Protection Clause

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Type** | Broad-Based Weighted Average |
| **Trigger** | Down Round (financing below €600/share) |

### 6.2 Formula

```
Adjusted Price = ((Pre-Money + New Money) / (Old Shares + New Shares))
```

### 6.3 Exceptions

No adjustment for:
- ESOP issuances (up to 10%)
- Strategic partnerships (with consent)
- Convertible loans at market terms

## 10. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

### 10.1 Lock-up Period

| Party | Lock-up | Exceptions |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Founder | 24 months | None |
| Investors | 12 months | Transfers to affiliates |

### 10.2 Right of First Refusal (ROFR)

On intended sale:
1. Seller first offers to the Company
2. Then to existing shareholders pro rata
3. Exercise period: 30 days

### 10.3 Tag-Along (Co-Sale Right)

If Founder sells >25% of their shares, Investors may co-sell on the same terms.

### 10.4 Drag-Along

On sale of the Company (>75% approval), all shareholders must sell on the same terms.
