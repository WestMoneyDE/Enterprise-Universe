# BEST PRACTICES

## Do's âœ…

| Rule | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Personalize** | Research the investor and their portfolio companies |
| **Keep it short** | First email max 150 words |
| **Use numbers** | Concrete metrics are more convincing than adjectives |
| **Clear CTA** | One clear call-to-action per email |
| **Follow up** | 3-4 follow-ups are normal and accepted |
| **Timing** | Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM is optimal |

## Don'ts âŒ

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| **Long emails** | Won't get read |
| **Too many attachments** | Spam filters, overwhelming |
| **Exaggerations** | "Revolutionary", "Disruptive" without proof |
| **Showing desperation** | Weakens negotiating position |
| **Over-following up** | Max once per week |
| **BCC mass emails** | Get recognized and ignored |
